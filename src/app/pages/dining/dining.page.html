Allegro Restaurant is Bluewater Maribago's main restaurant. It offers al fresco dining by the poolside patios. The restaurant is open 24 hours daily and is popular for its ala carte and buffet menu options. Enjoy its nightly themed dinners especially the Barrio Fiesta dinner every Saturday night where guests can immerse in local culture through live cultural presentations.

The Cove is a live seafood restaurant that offers panoramic view of the Bluewater private island and the open sea. Cool and breezy, this premier dining outlet is one of Cebu's best places to indulge in the freshest bounty of the sea. Experience a culinary journey that goes beyond fresh.

The Cove is open for lunch from 11:00 am to 3:00 pm and dinner starting at 6:00 pm until 10:00 pm daily.
